WARRANTY COVERAGES
------------------

1. BUMPER-TO-BUMPER (New Vehicle Limited Warranty)
   Coverage period: 3 years / 36,000 miles (whichever comes first)
   Status: EXPIRED
     - Time: Vehicle is now 4+ years old (expired August 14, 2024)
     - Mileage: Vehicle is at 47,250 miles (expired at 36,000 miles)
   What it covered: All factory-installed components except normal wear items
     (brake pads, wiper blades, tires, filters, belts, clutch, light bulbs)
   Excluded: Damage from accidents, misuse, modifications, or environmental causes

2. POWERTRAIN WARRANTY
   Coverage period: 5 years / 60,000 miles (whichever comes first)
   Status: ACTIVE
     - Expires: August 14, 2026 OR at 60,000 miles (whichever comes first)
     - Miles remaining on mileage limit: 12,750 miles (currently at 47,250)
     - Time remaining: Approximately 17 months
   What it covers:
     - Engine: All internally lubricated parts, engine block, cylinder head,
       timing chain, valvetrain, seals and gaskets
     - Transmission (10-speed automatic): All internal parts, torque converter,
       seals, gaskets
     - Drive axles, driveshaft, differential
   Important conditions:
     - Full synthetic oil (5W-30, Ford spec WSS-M2C946-B1) must be used
     - Oil change intervals (5,000 miles / 6 months) must be followed
     - Using non-Ford-approved fluids may void coverage
     - Modifications to engine, transmission, or drivetrain void powertrain warranty

3. CORROSION (Rust-Through) WARRANTY
   Coverage period: 5 years / unlimited miles
   Status: ACTIVE (expires August 14, 2026)
   What it covers: Perforation of sheet metal panels from rust originating
     from inside the panel outward
   Does NOT cover: Surface rust, cosmetic oxidation, rust from damage

4. EMISSIONS WARRANTY (Federal requirement)
   Coverage period: 8 years / 80,000 miles
   Status: ACTIVE (expires August 14, 2029 OR at 80,000 miles)
   What it covers: Major emissions components (catalytic converter, ECM/PCM,
     oxygen sensors)

5. SAFETY RESTRAINT WARRANTY
   Coverage period: 5 years / unlimited miles
   Status: ACTIVE (expires August 14, 2026)
   What it covers: Seat belts and supplemental restraint systems (airbags)

6. FORD ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE
   Coverage period: 5 years / 60,000 miles
   Status: ACTIVE (expires August 14, 2026 OR at 60,000 miles)
   Coverage: Towing, flat tire, fuel delivery, battery jump-start, lockout service
   Phone: 1-800-241-3673

WHAT IS NOT COVERED BY ANY WARRANTY
-------------------------------------
- Normal wear items: brake pads/shoes, wiper blades, tires, filters, bulbs, fuses
- Damage from accidents, collision, fire, theft, flood, or acts of nature
- Damage from misuse, racing, overloading beyond GVWR
- Damage from using incorrect fuel type or fluids
- Aftermarket accessories or modifications not approved by Ford
- Routine maintenance services (oil changes, tire rotations, etc.)
- Cosmetic damage: scratches, dents, paint chips from road debris

HOW TO FILE A WARRANTY CLAIM
------------------------------
1. Contact any authorized Ford dealership service department.
2. Bring this warranty documentation and vehicle identification.
3. Ford dealership will diagnose and contact Ford Motor Company for approval.
4. For disputes: Ford Customer Relationship Center: 1-800-392-3673
